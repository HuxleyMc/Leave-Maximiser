## The Secret? Strategic Booking ğŸ“…

Public holidays are free days off. The trick is to book your annual leave *around* these public holidays and weekends to create long, continuous blocks of time off while using as few leave days as possible.

For example, if a public holiday falls on a Tuesday, taking just the Monday off gives you a 4-day weekend for the "cost" of 1 annual leave day. That's a 4x Return on Investment (ROI)!

Doing this math manually across a whole year is tedious. **Leave Maximiser does it for you instantly.**
